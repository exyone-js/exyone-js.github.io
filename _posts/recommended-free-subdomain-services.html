mydns.xin

  • 后缀：.xin

  • 状态：站长自主开发，尚未上线，可以期待一下。

  • 备注：有点可惜没用上中国的.cn国别域名。虽然大家都吐槽.cn强制实名，想要私人信息安全就得“交保护费”，但很多知名二级域名服务确实都用自己国家的国别域名。

mydns.jp

  • 后缀：.jp（日本国别域名）

  • 特点：老牌服务商，安全可靠，有英语界面。

  • 注意：需要绑定信用卡。顺便一提，站长的mydns.xin和这家纯属巧合撞车，站长觉得.xin便宜就买了mydns.xin。

bluu.pl

  • 后缀：.pl（波兰国别域名）

  • 特点：我最推荐的一个！质量可靠，完全免费，且只需邮箱即可注册，不绑定任何私密信息，简单干脆，唯一的缺点就是网站只有波兰语，这倒不是啥大问题，随便找个翻译网站丢进去就行。

pp.ua

  • 后缀：.ua（乌克兰国别域名）

  • 特点：比较知名的免费服务，有英语界面。

  • 注意：需要绑定信用卡。另外，由于小乌局势严峻，服务稳定性可能受影响，建议观望。至于绑卡的事，在程序员圈子里，找个海归朋友帮忙绑个又不啥难事。

English-language version

mydns.xin

  • Suffix: .xin

  • Status: Independently developed by the site owner, not yet launched—something to look forward to!

  • Note: It's a bit of a shame it doesn’t use China’s .cn country-code domain. While everyone complains that .cn requires real-name registration (want privacy? “pay the protection fee” 😅), many well-known subdomain services do actually use their own country’s domain.

mydns.jp

  • Suffix: .jp (Japan’s country-code domain)

  • Features: A long-established provider, secure and reliable, with an English interface.

  • Note: Requires linking a credit card. By the way, the site owner’s mydns.xin coincidentally matches this one—they just bought it because .xin was cheap! 😄

bluu.pl

  • Suffix: .pl (Poland’s country-code domain)

  • Features: My top recommendation! Reliable, completely free, and only an email is needed to register—no private info required. Simple and straightforward! The only downside is the site is in Polish only, but that’s no big deal—just toss it into any translation site. 😎

pp.ua

  • Suffix: .ua (Ukraine’s country-code domain)

  • Features: A fairly well-known free service with an English interface.

  • Note: Requires linking a credit card. Also, due to the severe situation in Ukraine, service stability might be affected—recommend keeping an eye on it. As for linking a card, in techie circles, it’s no big deal to ask a friend who’s studied abroad for a little help. 😉
