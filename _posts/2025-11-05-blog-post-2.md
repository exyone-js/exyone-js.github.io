## Article Summary

This article recalls the childhood joys of the Red and White Console (FC/NES) era, describing a time without smartphones where games like Mario and Contra offered simple, pure happiness and friendship. It provides methods for customizing controllers and saving game progress, and lists several classic FC games including Tank Battle and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

### 1. Foreword

In those days without smartphones or the internet, the Red and White Console carried our entire childhood. Every time the school bell rang, we couldn't wait to rush home, plug in the game cartridge, and dive into that pixelated fantasy world. Mario's jumping figure, the tense battles of Contra, the fierce confrontations in Tank Battle – these classic games not only brought us endless fun but also became our precious childhood memories.

That time was simple and pure. Gathering with friends in front of the TV, we rejoiced over clearing a tiny level and got excited about defeating a single Boss. For us, the Red and White Console was not just a vehicle for games, but also a witness to youthful friendships.

Now, time flies and technology advances rapidly, yet the Red and White Console games in our memories remain the warmest imprints in our hearts. They represent not only that unique era but also the most sincere emotional symbols of our childhood.

If you have a particular game you'd like to revisit, feel free to leave a comment. I'll do my best to bring these classics back for everyone.

### 2. Game Instructions

    Click the controller icon in the bottom toolbar to customize keyboard keys or configure gamepad buttons.

    Click the disk icon in the bottom toolbar to save the game progress to a local directory. You can import the save file next time to continue playing.
